# Freshness Priority

Relevant, authoritative items published or materially updated inside 48 hours receive high editorial priority. Relevant items inside seven days are normally worth reviewing. Older items become low priority unless an evidenced rollout or material development is continuing.

The system stores the source publication date, source update date, discovery time, age in hours, priority reason, continuing-development flag, review time, and news-sitemap eligibility. Freshness is an editorial signal, not a universal expiry rule or ranking probability. Older ongoing rollouts and valuable evergreen work remain eligible for the normal sitemap.
