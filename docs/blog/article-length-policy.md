# Article length policy

Length follows article type: urgent news and explainers target 700-1,200 words; news analysis 1,200-2,000; checklists 1,000-1,800; evergreen and troubleshooting guides 1,500-2,500; technical guides 2,000-3,500; comparisons 1,200-2,400.

These are editorial ranges, not ranking scores. Useful shorter work is not padded or rejected solely for being below 1,500 words. Filler, low-information padding, repetition, and unsupported claims remain publication blockers.
