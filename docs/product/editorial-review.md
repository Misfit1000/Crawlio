# Editorial Review

AI-assisted drafting is optional and restricted to administrators. Generated text is saved as a draft and never publishes automatically.

Before scheduling or publishing, an administrator must confirm:

- the article answers a specific reader problem;
- factual and technical claims are accurate;
- links and source attribution are valid;
- no statistics, quotations, customers, or case studies were invented;
- ranking or traffic outcomes are not guaranteed;
- repetition and keyword stuffing were removed;
- limitations and relevant exceptions are included;
- the title, description, slug, canonical, and publication date are correct.

Changing article content clears the review acknowledgement.
