# SEOIntel Brand Voice

SEOIntel writes like an experienced auditor explaining evidence to a client: direct, calm, precise, and useful.

## Principles

- Lead with the finding or action, not a promotional claim.
- Explain technical terms the first time they appear.
- Prefer short headings and specific verbs.
- State limits beside the feature they affect.
- Never promise rankings, traffic, revenue, or guaranteed outcomes.
- Avoid filler such as "unlock", "powerful", "advanced", "seamless", and "all-in-one".

Audit findings and scores are produced by deterministic checks. Optional AI-assisted blog drafting is available only to administrators and always requires editorial review.
